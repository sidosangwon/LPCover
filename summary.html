Cover - Photo Editor by Limepresso

Key 6 Features

Simple swipe-based filter and template change

Users can slide left or right to switch between filters, and slide up or down to switch between templates.

15 Original Filters

Each filter was inspired by the best magazine covers around the world and carefully crafted to make the overlays stand out.

272 Templates

Variety of beautiful cover designs from simple ones to more elaborate combinations such as magazine covers. Cover collection has 7 categories (Hand-drawn, Love & Anniversary, Typeface, Inspiration, Travel, Frames, Layouts). All elements of each cover designs are fully adjustable.

115 Fonts & Text Adjust

Choose from a wonderful collection of fonts from some of the best designers in the world. You can size, rotate, fade, and center your text. Text can be adjusted with alignment, line-height, and letter-spacing. Color choices are hand-picked 51 types.

578 Artworks

Over 500 different stickers from doodles to geometric frames. Artwork section has 7 categories (Anniversary, Everyday, Travel, Nature, Lines, Shapes, Frames). You can size, rotate, fade, and center your sticker. Color choices are hand-picked 51 types.

Shuffle through filters and templates

The shuffle button will automatically create a combination of filters, brightness and template designs. Once a users find the cover combination that suits their photos, they can either tweak it further or share as it is.
